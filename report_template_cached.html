2.2. Charts

Chart 1: Revenue & Pre-tax Profit by Year (YTD 2024 vs 2025)

2024: Q2 revenue was the lowest, while Q3 & Q4 were peak revenue seasons.

2025: The cycle has shifted

  • Q2 has become the strongest quarter for revenue.
  • Q3 2025 revenue was lower than Q3 2024 by ~4.7%, although YTD is still up thanks to Q1-Q2.
Chart 2: Pre-tax Profit by Quarter

Cyclically, the peak is still around Q2-Q3, meaning the revenue 'Seasonality' still exists, but the profit seasonality has improved significantly:

  • Q2 is no longer a 'profit void'.
  • Q3 has become a quarter with both high revenue and excellent profit margins.
